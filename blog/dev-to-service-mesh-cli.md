## What I Built

**service-mesh-cli** — validates Istio/Linkerd mesh configs:

- **10 Validation Rules** — MESH-001 to MESH-010
- **mTLS, Circuit Breakers, Retry Policies** — Security & resilience checks
- **Traffic Weight Validation** — Canary/blue-green weight correctness
- **Health Score & Grade** — A-F grading (0-100)
